Projects and Research

  • Impact of ICT on Nigeria Economic Growth and Development (Nov 2019)

    Final Year Project, supervised by Prof. Benjamin S. Aribisala, submitted to the Computer Science Department, Lagos State University, Ojo, Lagos, Nigeria.

    Analyzed the impact of Nigeria's population, inflation, and GDP on the number of internet users in Nigeria using secondary data sourced from the World Bank. Provided empirical evidence showing the positive impact of ICT on Nigeria's economic growth and development using a multiple linear regression model. Recommended that the Federal Government should allocate more funds for the realization of an ICT-driven society and master plan for Nigeria.
